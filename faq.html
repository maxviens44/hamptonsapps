Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about using Turnover as a host or cleaner.

Getting Started
After signing up, tap the + button on the Properties tab. Enter your property name and address, add a cover photo, and set your timezone. You can then add rooms and assign cleaners.
Open your property, go to the Cleaners section, and tap Share Invite Code. You can send the 6-character code directly or share a QR code. Your cleaner enters the code in the Turnover app to join. You can also share the link — it opens the app automatically.
Go to your property and tap Rooms. Add each room with a name and icon, then tap into a room to build its task list. You can use pre-built templates (bedroom, bathroom, kitchen, etc.) or create custom tasks. Set each task as Must Do, Standard, or Check priority.
Scheduling
Open your property and tap Scheduled Cleans, then tap + to create a new clean. Set the date, time, and optionally assign a cleaner and add notes. You can also set a recurring schedule — weekly, bi-weekly, or monthly — which creates 12 future instances automatically.
Yes. Go to Property Settings and tap Calendar Sync. Paste your Airbnb or VRBO iCal feed URL and Turnover will automatically create a clean on every checkout day. You can still edit individual cleans after they're created.
Live Progress & History
When a cleaner starts a clean, you'll see an Active Clean indicator on your property dashboard. Tap it to open the Live Progress view — you can see which rooms are done, which are in progress, and the overall completion percentage in real time.
Go to your property and tap Clean History. Each submitted clean shows the cleaner's name, date, duration, room-by-room task completion, photos taken during the clean, and any damage reports filed.
Damage Reports & Supplies
Cleaners can file a damage report during or after a clean — they attach a photo, select the room, set severity, and add a description. You'll see all damage reports on your property dashboard. You can mark each one as resolved and add a repair cost, which is tracked in Insights.
Add supplies to your property (name, description, quantity) in the Supplies section. During a clean, your cleaner can mark items as low or out. You'll see the supply status on your property dashboard so you know when to restock.
Subscriptions & Plans
  • Free Trial (30 days) — Unlimited
  • Starter ($4.99/mo) — Up to 2
  • Pro ($9.99/mo) — Up to 5
  • Portfolio ($19.99/mo) — Up to 10
  • Unlimited ($29.99/mo) — Unlimited
Insights — including cleaning spend, average clean duration, and damage cost tracking — is available on Pro, Portfolio, and Unlimited plans.
Subscriptions are managed through the App Store. Go to iPhone Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions → Turnover, and tap Cancel. You'll retain access until the end of the current billing period.
After 30 days, you'll be prompted to choose a plan to continue. Your properties, rooms, and clean history are preserved — nothing is deleted. Cleaners are always free and are never affected by the host's subscription status.

Getting Started
Ask your host to share the property invite code or QR code. In the Turnover app, tap Join a Property on your home screen or in Profile, enter the 6-character code, and you'll be added instantly. You can also scan the QR code with your camera — it opens the app automatically.
Yes, completely free. Cleaners are never charged. Only hosts have a subscription.
Your home screen shows a digest of all cleans scheduled in the next 7 days across all your assigned properties.
During a Clean
Open the property and tap Start Clean. You'll see all rooms with their task lists. Work room by room, checking off tasks as you go. When a room is done, tap Mark Room Done to move on.
Each task has one of three priorities set by the host:
  • Must Do — Critical, never skip
  • Standard — Regular cleaning tasks
  • Check — Inspect or verify condition
Inside the active clean, tap the Photos tab. You can take a photo with your camera or select one from your library. Photos are attached to the clean and visible to the host when you submit.
Tap the Damage tab inside the active clean, then tap +. Select the room, attach a photo, set severity, and add a description. You can also file a damage report outside of an active clean from the property screen.
Inside the active clean, tap the Supplies tab. Tap any item to mark it as low or out. The host will see the status update on their dashboard.
Once all rooms are done, tap Mark House Ready. This notifies the host immediately. You'll then have the option to complete the final inspection checklist before submitting. The host receives the full clean report including tasks, photos, and any damage filed.
Team Cleans
Start a clean and tap Team Clean to generate a team code. Share the 6-character code with your crew members. They enter it in the app to join the same session. You'll all see each other's progress in real time, and rooms are locked so two people don't work the same room at once.
Crew members are helpers who join your active clean using a team code. They don't need their own Turnover account — they sign in anonymously through the app. Their work is merged into the clean when you submit.
Your progress is saved automatically. Open the property and tap Resume Clean to pick up exactly where you left off.
