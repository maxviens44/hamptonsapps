Turnover App

Every clean, perfectly coordinated

Hosts set up properties and monitor cleans in real time. Cleaners execute room-by-room checklists, submit photos, and flag damage — all in one app.

📋
Room-by-room checklists
Custom tasks per room with priority levels. Cleaners check off as they go — nothing gets missed.
📸
Photo documentation
Cleaners submit photos for every room. Hosts can review them anytime from session history.
⚠️
Instant damage reports
Cleaners flag damage with a photo and severity level. Hosts are notified immediately.
📊
Live progress tracking
Watch every clean unfold in real time. See which rooms are done and what's left.
💬
Built-in messaging
Hosts and cleaners communicate per property — no more texts lost in a thread.
📅
Scheduled cleans
Schedule one-time or recurring cleans. Cleaners always know what's coming up.
